About This Project

A music genre classifier powered by a custom convolutional neural network made from scratch, capable of analyzing and identifying musical patterns across ten genres.

Built using TensorFlow, this project can analyze audio files and determine their musical genre with a decent accuracy.

The classifier is trained on a dataset of music spanning ten distinct genres and predicts the results using melspectrograms of the audio files
